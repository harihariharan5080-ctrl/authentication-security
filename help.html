Operator manuals and guidance tips for CRT Face ID Attendance console.

Biometric Scanner Manual

Go to the Register Employee panel, fill out the form metadata (Name, ID, Department, Designation, and Date of Joining), and click "Start Camera". Stand in front of the camera. The biometric console will automatically start capturing frames when a face is detected. Adjust your face based on the guidance prompt at the bottom of the screen (Look straight, Look Left, Look Right, etc.). Once 30 frames are captured, the server compiles the template.

Ensure the webcam is connected to the laptop/desktop. Check the device permissions in your browser to verify that the website has access to the camera hardware. If index conflicts occur, navigate to Settings > Camera Controls and try changing the index selection from 0 to 1.

The CRT terminal operates strictly as an offline node for maximum laboratory security. All captured facial datasets and check-in ledgers are stored locally in the server's workspace folder (e.g. `dataset/` and `attendance/` directories) and are not synced with external networks.
Quick Help
Optimal Capture Conditions: Ensure the subject's face is evenly lit. Avoid strong backlights or shadow casting. Remove glasses and caps during the registration process for higher recognition accuracy.
Contact Security Admin: If you require profile data modifications or system resets, contact: admin@crt.lab.internal
